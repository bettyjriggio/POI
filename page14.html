Vindaloo Curry 

with pork or mixed vegetables

INGREDIENTS:

 2 small onions, diced
 1 cup of canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed
 OR ½ pound of cubed pork, cooked in a cup of water until tender (cover the pot whilst cooking and keep the
stock after)
 ¼ cup of potatoes, diced into about ½ inch cubes and washed
 ¼ cup of frozen peas, thawed and rinsed
 ¼ cup of carrots, peeled and diced into small cubes
 1 tablespoon of Vindaloo Curry Paste (add a little more from stronger or less for a milder taste)
 1 cup of canned diced tomatoes or fresh chopped tomatoes 

INSTRUCTIONS:

1. In a small pot, add a tablespoon of oil and let it warm
2. Add onions, and turn the burner on low once slightly golden
3. Add the potatoes, cook for 3 to 4 minutes on low to medium heat
4. Add the Vindaloo curry paste and mix well on low heat
5. Add chickpeas (or pork), turn heat to medium and cook for 2-3 minutes
6. Add carrots and peas and cook for 2-3 minutes
7. Lower the heat and then add tomatoes (add the pork stock just before), cover and let simmer for
about 15 minutes

Add a dash of salt and pepper for taste. Serve on a bed of rice, with flat bread or both. Enjoy!
