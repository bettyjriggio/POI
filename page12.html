Our Vision

The Port of India’s vision is to build a company focused around sustainability. In using local products and ingredients derived from a responsible supply chain, we have a strong commitment to having a positive environmental and social impact, with a focus on zero waste and using renewable energy. Creating an end-product that ethically-conscious consumers can be proud to support and use. 

The majority of ingredients used in The Port of India products and prepared foods are organic and locally sourced, with nearly all suppliers being Quebec-based in order to help support the local economy. The company aims to incorporate and use as many locally produced ingredients whenever possible. 

Our commercial kitchen runs solely on electricity from Hydro-Québec, generated primarily from water and wind, and not natural gas. All containers for prepared foods and bakery items are 100% compostable.  All products and prepared foods are also vegan.
