Green Masala Curry 

with tofu and spinach

INGREDIENTS:

 2 small onions, diced
  ½ pound of firm tofu, seasoned with lemon juice, pepper and salt, coated with a little flour and
sautéed before adding to the curry
 OR ½ pound of cubed lamb covered and cooked in ½ cup of water until tender (cover the pot whilst cooking
and keep the stock after)
 ¼ cup of potato, diced into small cubes and, washed, and drained
 ¼ cup frozen spinach chopped or fresh spinach washed and chopped
 1 tablespoon of Green Masala Curry Paste (add a little more for stronger or less for a milder taste)
 ¾ cup of coconut milk

INSTRUCTIONS:

1. In a small pot, add a tablespoon of oil and let it warm
2. Add onions, and turn the burner on low once slightly golden
3. Add the potatoes, cook for 3-4 minutes with low to medium heat
4. Add the Green Masala curry paste and mix well on low heat
5. Add the tofu (or lamb), turn heat to medium and cook for 4-5 minutes
6. Add the spinach, and wait until wilted
7. Lower the heat and then add the coconut milk (add the lamb stock just before) and let simmer for 5
minutes

Add a dash of salt and pepper for taste. Serve on a bed of rice, with flat bread or both. Enjoy!
